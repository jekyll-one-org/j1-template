[role="dropcap"]
The *ChatBot* is a built-in AI assistant that can answer your
questions about J1 Template directly on the website. It is powered by Claude,
an AI model developed by Anthropic. You do not need an account or any
setup — just type a question and get a helpful answer.

The ChatBot knows about all J1 Template features, including the video
player, playlists, import and export, series support, and more. It draws on
a dedicated knowledge base that is updated with each release.


[role="mt-5"]
== What the ChatBot Can Do

The ChatBot is designed to help you get the most out of J1 Template. You
can ask it about any feature or topic related to the website. Typical
questions include:

* How do I import a playlist?
* Which video collections are available?
* How does the merge toggle work?
* Can I use J1 Template on my phone?
* What player controls are available?
* How do I export and share my playlist?

The agent answers in plain language and, where helpful, includes
step-by-step instructions. It can respond in *any language* — simply
write your question in German, French, Spanish, or whichever language
you prefer, and the ChatBot will reply in the same language.


[role="mt-5"]
== How to Use the Chat

Using the ChatBot takes just a few simple steps:

. Navigate to the *ChatBot* page. You can find it in the main menu
  under *ChatBot > ChatBot*.
. You will see the chat window with a text input field at the bottom.
. Type your question or message into the input field that reads
  _Enter your message ..._.
. Press kbd:[Enter] on your keyboard or click the *send button* (the
  arrow icon to the right of the input field) to submit your message.
. Wait a moment while the ChatBot processes your request. You will
  see a brief loading indicator while it works.
. The agent's reply will appear in the chat window above your message.

You can continue asking follow-up questions in the same conversation.
The ChatBot remembers the context of earlier messages within the
current session, so you can refine or build on previous answers without
repeating yourself.


[role="mt-5"]
== Chat Window Layout

The chat interface is divided into several areas:

Header::
At the top of the chat window, you will see the title *J1 Template ChatBot*
and a *Neuer Chat* (New Chat) button on the right side.

Message Area::
The large central area displays the conversation. Your messages appear
on one side with a person icon, and the agent's replies appear on the
other side with a robot icon.

Input Bar::
At the bottom, you will find the text field where you type your messages.
Next to it is the send button.

Status Bar::
Below the input field, a small status bar shows the current connection
state (a green dot means: the agent is connected with the AI server) and
which AI model is in use (default: _Claude Haiku 4_).


[role="mt-5"]
== Starting a New Conversation

If you want to start fresh and clear the current chat history, click
the *Clear Chat* button in the top-right corner of the chat window.
This removes all previous messages from the screen and begins a new
conversation.

[role="mt-4 mb-5"]
[CAUTION]
====
Starting a new chat, what *clears* the current *chat history*, can be
helpful when you want to ask about a *completely different topic* and
do *not need the context* from your earlier questions. 
====

[role="mt-5"]
== Getting Good Answers

The ChatBot works best when your questions are clear and specific.
Here are a few tips:

Be specific::
Instead of asking _"How does the playlist work?"_ try something like
_"How do I sort my playlist by duration?"_ The more precise your
question, the more useful the answer will be.

Ask one thing at a time::
If you have several questions, it helps to ask them one after another
rather than combining everything into a single long message.

Use your own language::
The ChatBot can understand and respond in many languages. You do not
need to write in English if that is not your preferred language.

Refer to earlier answers::
Because the agent remembers the current conversation, you can say things
like _"Can you explain that last step in more detail?"_ and it will
know what you mean.


[role="mt-5"]
== Limitations

While the ChatBot is very helpful, there are a few things to keep in
mind:

* The agent only knows about *J1 Template*. It is _not_ a general-purpose
  assistant for unrelated topics.
* It does *not* search the internet. Answers come exclusively from the
 _ J1 Template knowledge base_.
* Very long or complex messages may occasionally produce less precise
  answers. If that happens, try breaking your question into smaller
  parts.


[role="mt-5"]
== FAQ

Find below answers to common questions about the ChatBot.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Do I need an account to use the ChatBot?

No. The ChatBot is available to everyone without any login or
registration.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Is the ChatBot free?

Yes. Just like the rest of J1 Template, the ChatBot is *100{nbsp}% free*
to use.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Which AI model powers the ChatBot?

The ChatBot uses *Claude*, developed by Anthropic. The specific model
in use is shown in the status bar at the bottom of the chat window.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Can the ChatBot help me with things outside of J1 Template?

The ChatBot is designed specifically for J1 Template. While it is powered
by a capable AI model, its knowledge base is focused on J1 Template features
and usage. For general questions, you may want to visit
link://claude.ai[Claude, window="_blank"] directly.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Is my conversation private?

Your messages are sent to the Anthropic API for processing, but they are
not stored on the J1 Template server. The chat history exists only in your
browser while the page is open. Once you close or reload the page, the
conversation is gone.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Why does the agent sometimes take a moment to respond?

The ChatBot sends your message to an external AI service and waits
for a reply. Depending on the length and complexity of your question, and
on current server load, this can take a few seconds. The status bar will
indicate when the agent is working on your request.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Can I use the ChatBot on my phone?

Yes. The chat interface adapts to smaller screens automatically. On a
mobile device, the layout adjusts so you can type and read comfortably.
