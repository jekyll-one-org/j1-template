### `photoswipe-caption-plugin.js` (5 changes)

**1. `slideDestroy` handler — clear pending fade timeout (correctness)**
A `hideCaption`/`showCaption` toggle that fires moments before `slideDestroy` leaves a 50ms or 400ms `setTimeout` armed against the now-removed element. The callback would then mutate `style` on a detached node and keep the captured `slide` reference alive. Now cleared explicitly.

**2. `zoomPanUpdate` handler — biggest perf win (performance)**
This handler fires on every pointer/wheel/pinch tick, easily 60–120 times per second. The original called `hideCaption`/`showCaption`/`adjustPanArea` and wrote `style.transform` on every tick, even when nothing had changed. The optimised version caches `_wasZoomedIn` and `_lastYOffset` on `slide.dynamicCaption` and only touches the DOM when state actually transitions. Class-toggle avoidance and skipping the redundant transform write are the meaningful gains.

**3. `updateCaptionPosition` (clarity + minor perf)**
The original re-read `slide.dynamicCaption.element` four times and computed `imageHeight` even on the `aside` branch where it's unused. Now caches the element reference and only computes `imageHeight` inside the `below` branch.

**4. `onCalcSlideSize` cleanup (clarity)**
Removed the dead `} else ;` no-op (looked like minifier residue), flattened the nested mobile/auto/explicit type chain by one indent level, cached the element reference, and turned the sparse single-line comments into one short paragraph per non-obvious decision.

**5. `hideCaption`/`showCaption` (clarity only)**
Added a comment explaining the 50ms vs 400ms timeout dance — the magic numbers aren't obvious without context.

### `photoswipe-lightbox.js` (only the J1 auto-init extension at the bottom — upstream Babel output untouched)

**1. MutationObserver hot path (performance)**
The original `mutations.some(m => Array.from(m.addedNodes).some(...))` allocated a fresh `Array` per mutation record on every DOM change anywhere in the page. On a busy SPA this fires dozens of times per frame. Replaced with a labeled-break loop that iterates the `NodeList` in place, allocates nothing, and short-circuits on first match.

**2. Deduplicated JSON-attribute parser (clarity)**
`_autoInitReadOptions` and `_autoInitReadCaptionOptions` were near-identical 12-line functions reading different attribute names. Collapsed into a shared `_autoInitParseJSONAttr(el, attr)` helper, with the original two functions kept as one-line wrappers so existing call sites and any external references still work.

### `photoswipe-core.js` — deliberately not modified

It is 7,086 lines of Babel-transpiled output from upstream PhotoSwipe v5.4.4 (the `var _this$pswp; (_this$pswp = this.pswp) === null || _this$pswp === void 0 || _this$pswp.method()` patterns are the giveaway). Editing it has two problems: any change is wiped the next time the upstream bundle is re-built, and the optional-chaining gymnastics aren't really hand-tunable without the original ES source. Optimisations belong upstream in the source, not in the transpiled artefact. I'd recommend tracking the upstream repo and re-bundling rather than patching this file.
